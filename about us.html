About us ?

Our pets are special to us, and to take care of our pet's health, we do our best to feed them a nutritionally balanced diet. Captive raised pet animals and birds have compromised immunity in comparison to their wild counterparts. Tom and Birdy in collaboration with partners identified techno and socio-commercial feasibility of products, that's naturally beneficial of general well-being of house-hold pets (dogs, cats and Pet birds).

We provide Pets Solutions through various products to enhance the livelihood of beloved pets. In alignment with our mission, our core values are to develop Innovative solutions, delivering Safe and Effective products and to perform our duties with extreme honesty and integrity.
